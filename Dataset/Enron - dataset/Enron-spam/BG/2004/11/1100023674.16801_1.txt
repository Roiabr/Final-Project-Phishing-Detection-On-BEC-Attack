GLOBAL DIVERSIFIED INDUSTRIES, INC. ANNOUNCES<br>
&nbsp;SIX MONTH REVENUES TOP $4 MILLION<br>
<br>
<br>
-- Six Month Revenues Surpass Total Annual Revenues for 2004 --<br>
<br>
CHOWCHILLA, CA., November 8, 2004 – Global Diversified Industries, Inc. (OTC.BB: GDVI), focused on the modular building industry with emphasis on the education market, today reported that it has generated more than $4 million in revenue during its first six months of the fiscal year ending October 31, 2004. &nbsp;Total revenues for fiscal year 2004 totaled $3.8 million, showing Global Diversified’s ever-increasing market saturation in the California public school market.<br>
<br>
Global Diversified Chairman and CEO Phil Hamilton said: &nbsp;“We are pleased with the growth that our modular subsidiaries have sustained over the past year.” &nbsp;Mr. Hamilton added that “the Company’s sales force continues to build momentum into the winter season with substantial quote activity compared to this same period in 2003. &nbsp;We currently have more than $20 million in potential new orders quoted to school districts throughout California. &nbsp;We expect this momentum to escalate during the third and fourth quarters, especially pertaining to ‘custom’ permanent-type modular construction.”<br>
<br>
<br>
About Global Diversified Industries, Inc. <br>
<br>
Global Diversified Industries, Inc. is a holding company with two wholly owned subsidiaries, Global Modular, Inc. and MBS Construction, Inc. Both are engaged in the modular construction marketplace with an emphasis on educational projects. They incorporate the latest in construction software, allowing them to better manage projects incorporating cost vs. profit ratios, construction and manufacturing schedules, purchasing, receiving and other facets of industrial management. The Company's work is found both in Northern and Southern California, with numerous projects on budget for school systems throughout the state. <br>
